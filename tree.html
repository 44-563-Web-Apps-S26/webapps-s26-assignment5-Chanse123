When reducing the browser size, the tree names row (col-md) breaks first. This happens because the col-md columns only stay horizontal on medium and larger screens. When the screen becomes smaller than the medium breakpoint, they stack vertically. The col-sm-2 letters stay in one row longer because they are set to remain horizontal on small screens and up.
